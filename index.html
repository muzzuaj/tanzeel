About Me.

DOCUMENTARY FILMMAKER & PHOTOJOURNALIST

Tanzeel Ur Rehman is an award winning photojournalist and documentary film maker with a decade of experience working across Asia for many of the worldâ€™s top publications and TV networks.

He is based in London and New Delhi. He regularly covers news and in-depth feature stories, with a special focus on sensitive human interest stories, crimes against women and gender issues.

He has contributed work to clients and publications such as The Telegraph, Daily Mail, Mail Online, Grazia magazine, New York Post, The Sun, Fabulous magazine, Gulf News, and The Mirror. Other clients have included Action Against Hunger.

And his human interest work has been awarded by Johns Hopkins Center for Communication Programs, Baltimore, United States.

Tanzeel is known for his passion and creativity as a cameraman and director of photography and has worked for national and international production houses and directors, filming for Channel 5, Discovery, BBC, ITV and RTL.

Over the years he has worked with filming teams from the US, UK, Germany and India, as well as independent projects of his own. He is confident working both alone and in a team with a clear vision of how a story should be told through a lens.

He has a vast amount of experience working in sensitive and highly emotional environments. He has also covered charitable events, high profile interviews and medical surgeries.

He enjoys collaborating with clients from a selection of industries and their visual story telling.

Tanzeel also co-founded the press agency Cover Asia Press and is responsible for managing a team of photographers and videographers from across India providing images and footage to picture and video desks all over the world.
